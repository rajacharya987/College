Diploma Programs

Sushma Koirala Memorial Engineering College

Founded in 1997 AD (2057 BS), Sushma Koirala Memorial Engineering College is a technical institution situated in Itahari, Sunsari district of Nepal, close to Itahari Stadium. The college is affiliated with the Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training (CTEVT) for its Diploma programs. We offer three-year diploma courses such as Civil Engineering and Computer Engineering, each with 48 seats annually. Scholarships are available for economically disadvantaged students.

Diploma in Computer Engineering

A three-year program focusing on computer hardware, software, and networking. Graduates can pursue careers as computer technicians, network administrators, software developers, or IT support specialists.

Diploma in Civil Engineering

A three-year program covering the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure. Graduates can work as civil engineering technicians, construction supervisors, site engineers, or quality control inspectors.

Entry Requirements

Academic Level: A minimum GPA of 1.6 in the SEE, with specific grade requirements in core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, and English. Typically, at least a C grade in these subjects is required.

Entrance Examination: Students must pass an entrance examination conducted by the CTEVT. This exam tests basic knowledge in Mathematics, Science, and English.

Application Process: Submission of a completed application form along with required documents such as SEE mark sheets, citizenship certificate / Birth Certificate, recent photographs, and other relevant certificates.
